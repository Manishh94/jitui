Merryfield

Everyday Rewards: With Merryfield, you can earn "Everyday Rewards" or points by making organic purchases. It's like getting a little something back every time you shop for organic products.

Special Deals on Demand: Enjoy exclusive "Activated Special Offers" that save you money on organic goodies. It's like having access to secret discounts whenever you want.

Simple Receipt Scanning: Use your phone to "Scan Paper Receipts" effortlessly, so you never miss out on earning rewards.

Digital Receipts: Say goodbye to paper clutter. We keep track of your purchases with "Digital Receipts" that are easy to access and manage.

Gift Cards Galore: You can redeem your points for "Earning Gift Cards," turning your organic choices into extra spending money.

Merryfield is an app to help users eat and live organically by rewarding organic purchases. While organic food can be more expensive than conventional products, the Merryfield app is a simple way to gain “Everyday Rewards” or points that you can use later to get money back.

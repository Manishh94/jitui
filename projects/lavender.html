Lavender Farm

Book Instantly: Our app syncs expert schedules, so you can book an appointment in a jiffy. No more waiting – it's all about quick and easy bookings.

Know When They're Free: Always know when your favourite wellness expert is available. It's like having your own wellness planner.

Customise Your Profile: Create a profile that's just right for you. It's all about making your wellness experience personal.

Discover Services: Explore a bunch of wellness services to find what you like best and keep your health in check.

Join and Book Easily: Become a part of our wellness community with membership options and book your appointments regularly without any fuss.

Wellness Classes: Try out wellness classes and events to add some fun to your healthy journey.

For the Experts: Wellness trainers (providers) can easily manage their schedules, making it a breeze for them.

Get Paid Safely: Wellness trainers can use Stripe to get paid securely, so it's straightforward and safe.

Nantucket's wellness hub. Syncing expert schedules, the app offers real-time booking, enhancing healthy living. Explore services, request appointments, and prioritise your well-being. It has two types of users: Clients and Providers (Wellness Trainers), both users login managed within the app.

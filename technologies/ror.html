Ruby on Rails (RoR) is a compelling choice for web development due to its elegant and developer-friendly framework. It emphasises convention over configuration, enabling rapid development and easy maintenance. RoR benefits from a large and active community, resulting in a wealth of gems (libraries) and plugins for extending functionality. It's a robust, scalable solution that has powered numerous successful web applications.
